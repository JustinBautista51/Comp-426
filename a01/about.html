Information about Saber (Arthur)
Saber (セイバー, Seibā?) also known as king Arthur is one of the main characters of Fate/Zero and one of the three main heroines of Fate/stay night. She is the Saber-class Servant of Kiritsugu Emiya in the Fourth Holy Grail War and Shirou Emiya in the Fifth Holy Grail War. She is the Servant of Norma Goodfellow (possessed by Manaka Sajyou) in the Labyrinth Holy Grail War of Fate/Labyrinth. She is one of the Servants of Ritsuka Fujimaru of the Grand Orders conflicts of Fate/Grand Order.
Real life Arthur
Although the Arthur of real life is much less interesting and magical than the saber depicted in fate stay night, none the less he retains much of the similar qualities in his story. He continues to have pulled the sword from the stone at an early age, ruled britain, had a knights of the round table, and had his immortal weapon excalibur and avalon.
